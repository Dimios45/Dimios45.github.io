✨ What’s This Project About?

RLFFP is an exploration into the future of robot learning. Imagine training a robot in a virtual world and then dropping that same brain into a real robot arm—seamlessly. That’s what this project is all about: bridging the gap between simulation and reality while pushing the limits of reinforcement learning.

🌍 From Sim to Reality

Training in simulation is easy. Making it work in the real world is where things get tricky.

  • 📷 Camera alignment had to be near perfect.
  • 👁️ Visual perception didn’t always match between sim & real.
  • ⚙️ Hardware calibration kept us on our toes.

Tackling these challenges gave us insights into what it really takes to bridge two worlds. (Spoiler: it’s harder than it looks!)
